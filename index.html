GitHub Community Gitam
GitHub Community Gitam is one of the most active tech clubs at gitam. The club is not a part of just one Campus but all 3 campuses of gitam (Visakhapatnam, Hyderabad, Bengaluru). The club has both tech team and non-tech team. The community is founded on April 2020 and has hosted various events till date. The club is open to any gitamite and can join/follow our various socials to get updates regarding upcoming or on-going events.
